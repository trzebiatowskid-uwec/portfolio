About Me

Hi, I'm Danika!

I’m a third year student at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire and I study English, French, and Multimedia Communications. I have a certificate of biliteracy for French and I’m working towards becoming fully bilingual.

I also work for the UW-Eau Claire choral department! I assist in a little bit of everything, which can include organizing department events and festivals, communicating with other departments, and generally keeping track of large amounts of information.

School work and my job consume a lot of my time, but some of my hobbies include reading, drawing, crocheting, playing piano, and making soup!
